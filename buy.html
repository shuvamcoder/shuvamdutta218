The Labyrinth's Whisper

Enter the captivating world of "The Labyrinth's Whisper" by Shuvam Dutta, a mysterious and thrilling tale set in the enigmatic corridors of Brawen Middle School. When thirteen-year-old Sergei's dear friend, Diem, mysteriously vanishes, he embarks on a gripping quest with his loyal companions—Hughes, Kestral, and Rosalva—to uncover the truth behind the school's haunting secrets. Nestled amidst dense forests, ancient legends, and an unsettling atmosphere, the school holds the key to solving an age-old mystery, testing the limits of their friendship and courage. Will they decipher the labyrinthine enigmas in time and rescue Diem from the clutches of the unknown? Prepare to be spellbound by this unforgettable adventure filled with suspense, camaraderie, and the echoing whispers of a hidden truth.
