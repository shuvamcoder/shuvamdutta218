Brawen Middle School, nestled among dense forests, is a place steeped in mystery and legends. Its classic brick architecture and tall windows invite both curiosity and trepidation. Whispers of haunted corridors and a cursed treasure hidden in the nearby forest add an eerie aura to the school. With a history of unexplained disappearances, the sense of unease lingers in the air. However, it is within these shadowed walls that a group of determined students embark on a quest to unravel the secrets that haunt Brawen Middle School and find the truth that lies beneath its enigmatic facade.

Kaiya's Bakery is a delightful haven tucked away on a quaint corner of the town. Its charming exterior adorned with colorful flower boxes and a whimsical sign beckons passersby to step inside. As you enter, the heavenly scent of freshly baked goods envelops you, instantly putting a smile on your face. The cozy interior is adorned with rustic wooden tables and shelves lined with an array of delectable treats. From fluffy pastries to artisanal bread and mouthwatering cakes, every creation at Kaiya's Bakery is a work of edible art. The friendly staff, led by the warm-hearted owner Kaiya, greet customers with genuine warmth and passion for their craft. Whether you're in need of a quick pick-me-up or searching for the perfect centerpiece for a special occasion, Kaiya's Bakery is a haven where delectable delights and a warm atmosphere combine to create a truly memorable experience.

The Maze's

Obstacles

As their journey took them deeper into the maze, they encountered a towering wall that commanded their attention. It stood as a formidable barrier, reaching skyward with an air of ancient grandeur. The surface of the wall was adorned with an intricate tapestry of carvings and symbols, their meanings shrouded in mystery. The craftsmanship was exquisite, each detail etched with precision and care. The wall seemed to possess a silent wisdom, whispering tales of forgotten legends and lost knowledge. Its sheer magnitude evoked a sense of awe and reverence, leaving them in awe of the labyrinth's secrets that lay beyond. With every step closer to the wall, the weight of anticipation grew, as if the wall itself held the key to their progress. They stood before it, their gazes drawn to the intricate patterns and enigmatic symbols that adorned its surface. It was a testament to the labyrinth's enigmatic nature, a challenge that dared them to decipher its riddles and uncover the hidden truths that lay within.
